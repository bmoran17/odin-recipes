Soya Biryani

A delicious pressure cooked biryani with soya chunks and eggplant that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

Initial Spices

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 5 green cardamoms
  • 2 black cardamoms
  • 7 black cloves (+ 6 extra)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 star anise
  • 5 bay leaves (+ 6 extra)
  • 6 whole black peppers
  • 1/2 tsp heeng

Remaining Spices

  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp tumeric power
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste

Everything else

  • 3 medium sized red onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 large egglant, medium-sized cubes
  • 5 thai chilies, sliced in half
  • 6 cups of rice, washed
  • 2 tsp ghee
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 12 oz soya chunks

Steps

  1. Soak soya chunks in warm water to hydrate them. Leave them submerged and set this aside.
  2. Saute all the intial spices (don't include the extra) and the thai chilies with the ghee and oil.
  3. Once the cumin seeds start poping, we can add the onions and saute them.
  4. Going back to our soaked soya chunks, we squeeze all the water from them.
  5. When onions start to look brown/fried, we add the remaining spices, the squeezed soya chunks, our cubed egglant, and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. We now begin to add layers of rice topped with extra: 3 black cloves, 1/2 tsp ghee, and 2 bay leaves. We also sprinkle each layer with a litle extra of garam masala, biryani masala, salt, and red chili powder.
  7. Pour water to until the rice is covered and pressure cook accordingly until rice is cooked.
